Hey there,

I'm Agostino. I'm interested in designing meaningful experiences that shape how people interact with technology. I'm currently finishing my master's degree in Digital & Interaction Design at Polimi where I delved into the word of UX.

Alongside my studies, I work as a part-time freelancer, designing and developing websites from scratch—blending strategy, aesthetics, and usability.

Previously, I earned a degree in Communication Design and have worked on projects that bridge visual storytelling and digital experiences. In 2023 participated in the Erasmus programme, studying for a semester at HfG in Germany (Schwäbisch Gmünd), delving for the first time into the field of Interaction design.

Beyond design, I'm drawn to climbing, football, and documenting moments through analog photography and filmmaking (look at this video I made!).
